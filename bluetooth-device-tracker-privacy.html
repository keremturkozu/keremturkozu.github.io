Privacy Policy

Bluetooth Device Tracker

Effective Date: May 14, 2025

Bluetooth Device Tracker respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Collected Data

Bluetooth Device Tracker does not collect any personal data (such as name, email, phone number).

The app only processes Bluetooth-related information such as signal strength, last seen time, and connection status for the purpose of displaying this data to the user within the app.
This data is never transmitted outside the device or shared with third parties.

2. Use of Data

Bluetooth Device Tracker uses Bluetooth signals only to help users find and connect to their Bluetooth devices.
No data is sent to any server or external system.

3. Third-Party Sharing

No data collected by the app is shared with third parties.

4. Advertising and Analytics

Bluetooth Device Tracker does not use any advertising or analytics services.
User behavior is not tracked or analyzed.

5. Data Storage and Security

User data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and UserDefaults.
This data is not encrypted and remains solely on your device for display purposes only.

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be available within the app or via this policy page.
